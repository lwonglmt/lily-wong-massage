Payment & Reimbursement

A clear note on insurance.

I do not bill insurance directly. However, many clients are reimbursed through HSA, FSA, or out-of-network benefits. Superbills are available upon request after each session.

  • FSA & HSA cards accepted

    Massage therapy may be eligible for FSA and HSA reimbursement. Most plans require a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) from your physician confirming massage therapy is medically necessary for your specific condition. Once you have it, I can provide a superbill with the clinical codes your administrator may need.

  • Superbill provided on request

    A detailed receipt with session information, clinical procedure codes, and license credentials — submit to your insurance company for out-of-network reimbursement, or use to document an FSA or HSA expense.

  • Cards, ACH, and Zelle

    All major cards. ACH or Zelle for packages. No surcharges either way.

  • Cancellations within 24 hours

    Less than 24 hours notice: 50% of session rate. No-shows: full session rate. Earlier reschedules — no fee.
